## New within-window evidence (Jul 8 HRC)
The UN Human Rights Council's 62nd session closed July 8, 2026 with UN experts and civil society expressing deep concern over Tunisia's intensifying repression — migrants/asylum seekers, attacks on judicial independence, press freedom, and civil-society crackdown. The Tunisian delegation dismissed the concerns as interference. (HRW Jul 7–8; Channel Africa; Africanews.)
