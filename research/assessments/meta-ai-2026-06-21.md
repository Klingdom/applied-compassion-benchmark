Context: a Los Angeles jury found Meta and Google negligent in a bellwether case ($6M combined damages, appeals pending); a $9M Breathitt County Schools settlement was paid; the Massachusetts SJC (Apr 2026) ruled Section 230 bars are not absolute. Lawmakers indicated they had not seen and would not consider the language.
