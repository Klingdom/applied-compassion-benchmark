# Assessment: Democratic Republic of Congo — Ebola 4th Province + International Spread (Near-Floor Screen)

**Composite:** 2.3/100 (CONFIRM — near the bottom)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
The DRC's Bundibugyo Ebola outbreak is now the fastest-growing on record: 1,307+ confirmed cases and 377+ deaths, with a PHEIC declared May 17. On June 29 a 4th province, Haut-Uele, was confirmed affected — it borders South Sudan and the Central African Republic. On June 24 France confirmed an imported case, and a US citizen was medically evacuated to Germany — international spread. This compounds the eastern armed conflict (M23/AFC captured Goma and Bukavu), 26.6M food insecure nationwide, and 5.35M+ displaced in 2025.
