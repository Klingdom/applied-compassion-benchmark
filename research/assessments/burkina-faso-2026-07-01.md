# Assessment: Burkina Faso — Lean-Season Peak (Double-Count / Calibration Screen)

**Composite:** 6.3/100 (CONFIRM)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
Burkina Faso is at the extreme end of a compounded crisis. It is the country most affected by terrorism globally (GTI 2026), with 2.1M+ internally displaced — the largest in the Central Sahel — and 3.5M+ people besieged by JNIM. Only 28% of a $3.7B humanitarian appeal is funded, and 401,000 children have severe acute malnutrition. In April 2026 the military authorities dissolved 118 NGOs, including human-rights groups — a civic-space collapse. As of July 1 the scan records no new specific development.
