# Assessment: Taiwan — First Formal Baseline (Attribution Screen)

**Composite:** 83.0/100 (CONFIRM)

## Why it matters (plain language)
Taiwan has never had a full assessment. It surfaced this cycle on military-pressure news: China Coast Guard patrolling its eastern waters continuously since June 1, and Taiwan commissioning a new Littoral Combat Command on July 1. Crucially, that pressure is China's conduct, not Taiwan's. Taiwan's own conduct — universal healthcare, Asia-leading rights, a robust democracy — is consistent with its Exemplary score. The defensive command is a legitimate civilian-protection measure, not a compassion harm.
