## Critical In-Window Event

**April 27-28 2026:** Google signed classified Pentagon AI deal allowing Gemini models for "any lawful government purpose" on classified networks (mission planning, weapons targeting). 580+ Google employees, including 100+ DeepMind researchers, signed letters opposing. Senior DeepMind researcher publicly stated "incredibly ashamed." A senior director and 20+ directors/VPs signed openly. Letter cited risk of "irreparable damage" and "AI systems centralize power and make mistakes." Google proceeded.

**Counter-signal (same week):** Google quietly exited a $100M drone swarm contest.

**Background context:** February 2025 — Google removed pledge from AI principles to avoid building weapons/surveillance, with Hassabis citing "global competition for AI leadership." Anthropic's previous Pentagon exclusion was the precedent that opened this contract opportunity for Google.
