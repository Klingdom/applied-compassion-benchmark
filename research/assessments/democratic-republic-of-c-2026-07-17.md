## Key Evidence (within-window)

- As of 2026-07-16 the DRC reported 2,124 confirmed Ebola cases including 828 related deaths, an increase of 51 confirmed cases and 32 deaths since the 07-15 report. Ituri province remains most affected with 1,904 cases and 692 deaths across 27 of 36 health zones. Source: https://www.ecdc.europa.eu/en/ebola-outbreak-democratic-republic-congo-and-uganda ; https://www.who.int/emergencies/disease-outbreak-news/item/2026-DON612

- The outbreak surpassed 1,000 confirmed cases within 40 days of response activation, against roughly 235 days for the 2018 North Kivu outbreak — making it the fastest-growing Ebola outbreak on record.

- Neighbouring Uganda discharged its last Ebola patient on 2026-07-16 and began the 42-day countdown to declaring its outbreak over — a genuine positive regional development, but attributable to Uganda, not the DRC.
