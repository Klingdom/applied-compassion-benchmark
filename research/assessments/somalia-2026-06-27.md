# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Somalia

**Assessment date:** 2026-06-27
**Composite score:** 4.7/100 (CONFIRM — near-floor reinforced)
**Band:** Critical

## Headline (plain language)

Why it matters: Somalia scores 4.7 of 100. Buur Hakaba district has crossed one famine threshold (acute malnutrition), but as of today no formal IPC famine has been declared — so the deepening crisis reinforces the near-floor without a new threshold crossing.

## §3e-bis Screening + Threshold Check
- **Baseline provenance:** Published 4.7 (AWR 1.5, EMP 1.5, ACT 1.5, EQU/BND/ACC/SYS/INT 1.0). Reconstructs to 4.7.
- **Threshold check (decisive):** The IPC end-of-June famine-threshold date for Buur Hakaba has passed. Independent verification (IPC/FSNAU reporting and the EU/JRC Famine Review) confirms Buur Hakaba is at IPC AMN Phase 5 (GAM 37.1% — one of three famine thresholds met) and faces a RISK of famine under a worst-case scenario, but **no formal IPC famine declaration has been issued** as of Jun 27. A formal famine declaration would require all three thresholds; that has not occurred.
- **Directionality:** Within-window evidence is negative and reinforces the near-floor; it is a compounding shock on an already-priced crisis. No new threshold crossing, so no scorable downgrade event. No positive countervailing own-conduct evidence.

## Within-window evidence
- **IPC (Jun 2026, Tier-5):** Alert — "Famine projected in two districts"; Buur Hakaba at IPC AMN Phase 5; risk of famine under worst-case scenario; no formal declaration issued. https://www.ipcinfo.org/ipcinfo-website/alerts-archive/issue-69/en/
- **EU JRC / Famine Review (2026, Tier-5):** Conclusions for Burhakaba and Baidoa confirm extremely critical malnutrition and famine risk — a risk projection, not an official declaration. https://knowledge4policy.ec.europa.eu/publication/somalia-famine-review-ipc-analysis-conclusions-recommendations-burhakaba-baidoa_en
- **Action Against Hunger / Oxfam (Jun 2026, Tier-2/3):** GAM 37.1%; most malnutrition facilities closed; WFP reaching only 1-in-10 in need; 6.7M facing high acute food insecurity; 1.9M children acutely malnourished. https://www.actionagainsthunger.org/press-releases/somalia-faces-deepening-hunger-crisis-as-risk-of-famine-emerges-in-burhakaba/

## Key Findings
- Verified: no formal IPC famine declaration as of Jun 27 — the threshold-crossing event that would justify a downgrade has not occurred.
- The deepening crisis reinforces the near-floor across ACT, EQU, ACC and SYS but is a continuation of an already-priced emergency.
- Confirmed at 4.7. No change proposal. Forward trigger: a formal IPC famine declaration for Buur Hakaba/Baidoa would warrant immediate re-assessment.
