# Assessment: Pakistan — First Full Baseline (Exogenous-Disaster + Double-Count Screen)

## Why it matters (plain language)
Pakistan has prior change proposals (May 2025) but no standalone full assessment file. It surfaced on catastrophic monsoon flooding — 6.9M affected, 1,000+ dead, ~3M displaced — plus accelerating Afghan refugee deportations and a new online-speech law. The flooding itself is a natural disaster (exogenous); only the government's response is scorable, and it remains weak but not newly, deliberately harmful. The deportations and speech restrictions are Pakistan's own conduct but were already priced in the May 2025 downgrade to 17.2. Net: confirm.
