## Critical In-Window Event

On **March 31 2026**, Oracle laid off approximately **30,000 employees (~18% of global workforce)** via 6:00 AM termination emails with **immediate Slack/system access revocation**. Employee monitoring software was reportedly installed pre-layoff. The action freed an estimated $8-10B for AI data center expansion. Oracle reported a 95% jump in net income same quarter ($6.13B). Class action firms (Strauss Borrelli PLLC) opened WARN Act investigations in Washington and Missouri. As of April 30, no lawsuits filed; investigations active.

This event dominates the in-window assessment for **EMP, BND, ACC, INT, EQU** dimensions.
