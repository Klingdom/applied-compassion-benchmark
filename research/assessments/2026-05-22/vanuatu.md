## Methodology Note — INT Governance Translation Pattern

Vanuatu now exhibits a sustained pattern: (1) international advocacy (UNGA lead sponsor) → (2) institutional outcome (UNGA resolution adopted) → (3) domestic translation (L&D Policy with budget). This is the strongest INT-dim signal a small state can produce — the alignment between international rhetoric and domestic implementation is precisely what I4 Values Alignment and I5 Resilience of Care anchors describe.

The cumulative uplift (+3.5) is conservatively below the 5pt apply threshold because:

1. Domestic equity/empathy dimensions still show Vanuatu's structural gaps (GBV prevalence, governance fragility).
2. L&D Policy is launched, not yet fully implemented (5-year horizon).
3. Boundary proximity (0.6 below Functional floor) warrants conservative anchoring; first crossing would require stronger evidence on the lagging dimensions or a third confirmation event.
