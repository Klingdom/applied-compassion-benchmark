## Context

First full assessment. Published composite 4.4 (Critical). **Significant political discontinuity in 2026**:

1. **Jan 3, 2026:** US military strike (Operation Absolute Resolve) captured Maduro and Cilia Flores; bombed air defenses in northern Venezuela; arraignment in Manhattan Federal Court Jan 5 on narco-terrorism charges ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_United_States_intervention_in_Venezuela), [House of Commons Library](https://commonslibrary.parliament.uk/research-briefings/cbp-10452/)).
2. **Jan 8, 2026 onward:** Acting President Delcy Rodríguez announced release of political prisoners; 621 released per Foro Penal as of Mar 8; 500+ remain ([NPR](https://www.npr.org/2026/01/12/nx-s1-5672535/venezuela-releases-political-prisoners-days-after-u-s-removes-nicolas-maduro), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026_political_prisoner_release_in_Venezuela)).
3. **UN Fact-Finding Mission (March 2026):** "Extensive legal and institutional machinery" enabling human rights violations remains intact; "at least 87 new politically motivated detentions" since Jan 3; pro-government civilian groups continue harassing opposition and journalists ([UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/03/1167126), [OHCHR](https://www.ohchr.org/en/press-releases/2026/03/venezuela-uncertainty-venezuela-must-give-way-meaningful-human-rights-change)).
4. **Amnesty International (2026):** Human rights defenders report released; continued government harassment documented ([Amnesty](https://www.amnesty.org/en/documents/amr53/0685/2026/en/)).

## Score Assessment Logic

The prior-Maduro composite (4.4) reflected severe state-level compassion failure. Since Jan 3:
- **Mild upward signal:** 621 political prisoners released (AB5 modest improvement, I1 modest).
- **Persistent critical signals:** 87+ new political detentions; structural persecution machinery intact; civil society still under threat.
- **New harm vector:** US intervention itself has caused displacement and uncertainty; sovereign disruption.

Net: Marginal upward revision from 4.4 to 7.8 (+3.4) — within measurement noise, but reflecting material prisoner-release event. Score delta below 5-point threshold for change proposal.
