# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Democratic Republic of the Congo (Near-Floor Confirmation)

**Published composite:** 2.3/100 (Critical) — rank 181, countries index.
**Determination:** CONFIRM at 2.3. Near-floor arithmetic forecloses a 5pt move. Epidemic toll is largely externally inflicted; a modest positive response signal is non-structural.

## Within-Window Evidence
The 2026 Bundibugyo Ebola outbreak reached **1,792 confirmed cases and 625 deaths as of July 9** — the deadliest Bundibugyo outbreak on record and third-largest Ebola outbreak overall. Ituri remains the epicenter (1,631 cases, 535 deaths across 25 of 36 health zones); spread to Tshopo and Haut-Uélé confirmed. WHO says the outbreak has not yet peaked ([UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/07/1167882); [Medical Daily](https://www.medicaldaily.com/ebola-bundibugyo-drc-625-deaths-us-entry-restrictions-july-21-2026-476040)).

## Attribution Analysis
- **Externally inflicted (natural epidemic):** Most of the death toll is the epidemic itself, not DRC own conduct.
- **Own-conduct aggravators (scored):** Poor health infrastructure and the government's redeployment of its army from Ituri to Kivu to counter the M23 rebellion enabled armed-group (ADF, CODECO, M23) expansion; Congolese army and armed actors hamper humanitarian access — worsening detection and containment ([ISS Africa](https://issafrica.org/iss-today/why-eastern-drc-s-peace-efforts-are-vital-to-containing-ebola); [Refugees International](https://www.refugeesinternational.org/reports-briefs/addressing-the-ebola-crisis-amid-the-humanitarian-emergency-in-eastern-drc/)). These aggravators are consistent with the already-priced near-floor baseline (eastern-DRC mass atrocities and displacement).
- **Modest positive:** A clinical trial (monoclonal antibody MBP134 + remdesivir) began July 2; the case rise partly reflects expanded community surveillance. This is externally-supported and non-structural — not a ≥5pt positive.

## Determination
From 2.3, a ≥5pt move up is arithmetically hard and unsupported by structural positive evidence; the net signal is negative. **CONFIRM 2.3.** Downward watch sustained.
