## Methodology Adjudication — Decision: DOCUMENTED HOLD (Tier C)

Applying the second-event-at-floor convention (Ruling 3, May 24) and the May 25 ruling on protest-mobilization-as-societal-response:

**Test 1 — Is the Izmir tear gas dispersal a categorically distinct trigger?**
The May 25 ruling enumerated qualifying triggers for a third-event apply: **(a) lethal force against protesters; (b) mass arrests (beyond carry-forward arrests); (c) formal CHP closure; (d) emergency declaration.** Tear gas and water cannon are non-lethal crowd-control tools used throughout the protest arc — they fall within the already-scored crackdown pattern, not above it. May 24 absorbed the precedent: physical force against CHP institutional space. May 26 extends that pattern to a second protest (Izmir). Pattern extension at floor-proximity does not trigger a new apply.

**Test 2 — Does the Güzelbahçe mayor detention add categorical novelty?**
The 20+ CHP-mayor detention count is itself the carry-forward arc that anchored the May 21 judicial-removal apply and the May 24 HQ-seizure apply. One additional mayor in the same legal theory (alleged corruption) does not categorically extend the arc — it is incremental accumulation within the same prosecution pattern. Score: no new apply.

**Test 3 — Does the 93-account social media block add categorical novelty?**
This is the most plausible candidate for a new categorical signal — it represents a discrete shift to digital speech suppression beyond physical/institutional measures. However: (a) 93 accounts is modest in scale relative to Turkey's documented history of platform-level blocks (X/Twitter throttling 2024, Wikipedia ban 2017-2020, etc.); (b) the carry-forward AWR floor (1.7) already reflects systematic information-environment suppression; (c) Turkey's existing AWR score (17.5 scaled) is already at Critical-band depth. Promoting this to a discrete apply would consume disproportionate downward space at floor-proximity for what is, in volume terms, a single-day modest blocking action.

**Test 4 — Cumulative arc weight (May 24-26).**
Combined three-day arc (HQ seizure May 24 + tear gas x2 May 24 & May 26 + 20+ mayors + 93 accounts + court-appointed leadership consolidation) represents intensification of an already-scored crackdown pattern. The May 24 apply (-2.5 with BND -5.0) and the May 23 apply (-4.9) already absorbed the arc's institutional core. Further apply absent categorically distinct evidence would risk double-counting.
