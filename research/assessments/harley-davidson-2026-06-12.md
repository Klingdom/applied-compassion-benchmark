## Context

Harley-Davidson (rotation) ended its DEI program (no DEI function since April 2024) after activist pressure, and conducted a corporate reduction-in-force in 2025 under a new CEO (USW members at Milwaukee/Menomonee Falls reportedly not impacted). On the positive side, 2024 voluntary turnover was the lowest in company history and engagement the highest since 2021. The DEI rollback modestly lowers Equity and Integrity (values-under-pressure); the strong retention/engagement keeps Boundaries intact. Net effect is a marginal -3.2 from the published 39.1 — a confirmation.
