## Context

DRC (priority 55) faces a "catastrophic collision of disease and conflict" (WHO): a Bundibugyo-strain Ebola outbreak in Ituri (confirmed May 15; the scanner reports 635 confirmed cases / 127 deaths by June 10, while WHO's May 27 figures cite 900+ suspected / 220+ suspected deaths with one lab-confirmed death) with no approved vaccine or treatment, spread across 11 health zones. ~10M face acute hunger Jan–June 2026. Ongoing MONUSCO-documented civilian attacks. The collision of epidemic and armed conflict confirms the near-floor published 2.3.
