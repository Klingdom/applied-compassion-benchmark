# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Nigeria (Confirmation)

**Assessment date:** 2026-07-10 | **Published composite:** 18/100 (Critical) | **Recommendation:** confirm

## Within-window signal
Worst northern hunger in nearly a decade continues: 36.2M nationally food insecure; 17M+ across nine northern states at crisis/emergency/catastrophe IPC levels (up ~2M); Borno epicenter with 3M+ acutely food insecure and 10,000+ at catastrophe level. WFP needs $89M over six months; funding shortfalls cutting coverage. (WFP, Leadership.ng.)

## Threshold determination
No material change from the 2026-07-09 record. This is a continuation of the already-priced famine crisis (the Jun-19 band-crossing event that placed Nigeria at 18). The driver is conflict (Boko Haram/ISWAP) plus donor funding collapse, partly external; the state's weak response is already reflected. Sub-threshold.

## Outcome
**Confirm at 18 (Critical). Downward watch sustained.**
