## Trigger for Reassessment

The Ukraine-Russia Orthodox Easter ceasefire (32 hours from April 11, 4pm) collapsed quickly, with both sides accusing the other of violations. The Kremlin stated it would not extend the truce unless Kyiv accepts Russia's territorial demands (all of Donetsk) — demands Kyiv called unacceptable. UN General Assembly adopted a resolution demanding immediate unconditional ceasefire on April 12 (the fourth anniversary of the war). US-facilitated peace talks remain stalled. The Easter ceasefire collapse is significant new evidence of the durability of hostilities and continued civilian harm.
