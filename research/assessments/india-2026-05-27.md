## Triggering Evidence Window

**Labour Codes implementation:**
- **2026-05-08 to 2026-05-09:** Central rules notified for the Four Labour Codes (Code on Wages, Industrial Relations Code, OSH Code, Social Security Code). Consolidates 29 prior laws. Source: https://topsourceworldwide.com/insights/indias-new-labour-law-reforms-2026-what-employers-need-to-know/
- **Critical structural concerns:**
  - Establishments with fewer than 300 workers excluded from core work protections (hours, leave, termination) — vast majority of India's industrial units exempted.
  - Strike notice requirement increased to 60 days + 14-day cooling-off period — spontaneous collective action effectively eliminated.
  - Wage offences settled by fee payment — illegality monetized.
- **Source (critical framing):** https://www.nationalheraldindia.com/opinion/new-labour-codes-modi-government-playing-with-fire
