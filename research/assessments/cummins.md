## Scoring Judgment (40 subdimensions condensed to 8 dimensions)

- **AWR 56.3 (Awareness):** Cummins' annual sustainability/destination-zero reporting acknowledges emission impacts and climate transition; the multi-year defeat-device deception however documents a structural awareness-disclosure gap — the company knew certified emissions did not reflect real-world operation. Functional.
- **EMP 56.3 (Empathy):** No documented systemic worker-mistreatment pattern in window; community-empathy gap is reflected in BND rather than EMP. Functional.
- **ACT 62.5 (Action):** Genuine product-mission delivery — power generation, transit, agriculture, marine; Accelera zero-emission pivot is a substantive transition investment. Functional-plus floor. ([Cummins Accelera](https://www.cummins.com/news/releases/2023/01/05/cummins-launches-accelera-cummins-power-zero-emissions-future))
- **EQU 50.0 (Equity):** Pollution-equity dimension: NOx exposure burden falls disproportionately on overburdened communities adjacent to highway corridors; this is the harm-distribution layer of the defeat-device episode. Plus April 16 Title VII sex-bias suit survival. Functional minus.
- **BND 37.5 (Boundaries):** Lowest dimension after INT. Population-level boundary violation: deliberate manipulation of emission controls on ~1M consumer vehicles raises NOx output above legal limits across multiple model years. Comparable in severity to Becton Dickinson's environmental tort exposure (BD -27.3 anchor). ([EPA enforcement](https://www.epa.gov/enforcement/2024-cummins-inc-vehicle-emission-control-violations-settlement))
- **ACC 50.0 (Accountability):** Settlement paid in full and recall is being executed; however, EPA records show prior recall and emissions-related episodes (2007, 2010, 2013) — accountability is reactive after enforcement rather than proactive disclosure. Functional.
- **SYS 62.5 (Systems):** Genuine systems-level positive — global engine and power-generation supply chain reaches public transit fleets, agriculture, healthcare emergency-power, and disaster-relief generators. Functional-plus floor. ([Cummins Accelera](https://www.cummins.com/news/releases/2023/01/05/cummins-launches-accelera-cummins-power-zero-emissions-future))
- **INT 31.3 (Integrity):** Lowest dimension and the lowest INT score in the Gen-2 forensic anchor series. Multi-year affirmative engineering of software to deactivate emission controls during normal driving is categorically more severe than misleading-statements (Danaher) or petition-to-dismantle-accountability (Lilly) patterns. The company built the deception into the product. ([EPA newsroom](https://www.epa.gov/newsreleases/united-states-and-california-announce-diesel-engine-manufacturer-cummins-inc-agrees))
