## Trigger for Reassessment

NYC Mayor Zohran Mamdani released the Preliminary Citywide Racial Equity Plan on April 6, 2026 — the first governmentwide racial equity framework in NYC's history. The plan sets data-driven agency goals across seven domains including Housing and Preservation, Community Safety/Rights/Accountability, Economy, and Health and Wellbeing. NYC also published a True Cost of Living Measure. NYC is the highest-ranked US city in the index, making it a benchmark-setting signal.
