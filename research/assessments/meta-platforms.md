### Score Difference Analysis

**EMP (-15.0 points):** The published score of 2.0 raw (25.0 scaled) implied some empathetic engagement. Since publication, Meta has eliminated its DEI programs, revised hate speech policies to explicitly allow targeting of LGBTQ+ people, and dismissed a $375M child safety verdict. These developments indicate empathy infrastructure has degraded significantly.

**ACT (-12.5 points):** The published score implied some responsive action. Meta's rollback of content moderation, elimination of fact-checking programs, and reduction of safety staff represent a material decline in compassionate action capacity.

**BND (-10.0 points):** Published score implied some boundary practices. The EUR 1.2B GDPR fine and ongoing privacy violations indicate consent and scope clarity are worse than the published score suggested.

### Recommendation

The published score appears slightly overstated based on current evidence, particularly in Empathy, Action, and Boundaries dimensions. However, the composite delta of -1.3 points is below the 5-point threshold for a change proposal. The published Critical band remains accurate.

## Key Findings

- Meta lost landmark child safety cases totaling $375M+ in damages with ~2,000 pending lawsuits, and its legal defense posture prevents any harm acknowledgment
- Sweeping rollback of content moderation, fact-checking, and DEI programs in January 2025 has weakened safety infrastructure across the platform
- EUR 1.2B GDPR fine (largest ever) for unlawful data transfers demonstrates systemic consent violations
- Hate speech policy revisions explicitly allow targeting of LGBTQ+ communities, representing a regression in equity protections
- Environmental sustainability program (net-zero by 2030, supplier engagement) is the only area showing forward progress

## Strongest Dimensions

- **Boundaries (15.0):** Some employee benefits and workplace structure exist, though consent practices are poor
- **Awareness (12.5) and Action (12.5):** Minimal infrastructure exists but is being actively degraded

## Weakest Dimensions

- **Equity (7.5) and Accountability (7.5):** DEI elimination and legal defense posture represent near-total absence of equity and accountability infrastructure
