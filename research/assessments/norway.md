## Trigger — Ukrainian Men 18-60 TCP Ban + Welfare Consultation

1. **Tier-1 in-window event**: March 27, 2026 — Norwegian government formally announced that men aged 18-60 will no longer automatically receive temporary collective protection (TCP). Applications received on or after May 5, 2026 are processed under standard asylum procedure on a case-by-case basis. ([Envoy Global](https://www.envoyglobal.com/news-alert/norway-new-restrictions-for-ukrainian-men-seeking-protection/); [Ukrainska Pravda](https://www.pravda.com.ua/eng/news/2026/02/26/8022915/))
2. **Tier-2 in-window event**: Late-April 2026 — consultation deadline on replacing social-assistance and housing benefits for refugees with a single, lower integration allowance for up to five years. ([Nordisk Post](https://www.nordiskpost.com/2026/01/24/norway-new-integration-allowance-refugees/))
