## Methodology Adjudication: Is EP-Urging-of-Conditionality a Tier-1 Apply or Tier-2 Accumulator?

**Methodology-novel ruling required.** The scanner flagged this as EU-CONDITIONALITY-MECHANISM-SECOND-ENTITY-PRECEDENT.

**Ruling — Tier-1.5 formal apply.** A 418-207 EP resolution urging conditionality is independently apply-threshold for the following reasons:

1. **Institutional weight.** The European Parliament is a co-equal EU institution. A formal plenary resolution adopted by a 4-group supermajority is qualitatively distinct from a Commission Article 7 procedural step. It is also qualitatively stronger than a single MEP statement, a committee report, or a political-group demand.
2. **Precedent equivalence to Hungary.** When EP took similar action against Hungary, the Commission subsequently fired conditionality mechanism, freezing €10.4B in RRF funds. The Hungary parallel is explicit and was made by EP rapporteurs themselves. Slovakia's published baseline of 33.6 already encodes the Liberties Dismantler classification (May 22 -5.5 apply); the EP escalation is a SECOND institutional confirmation cycle from a higher-tier source.
3. **Symmetric treatment with EC actions.** If EP urging produces no scored event, the benchmark would be implicitly waiting for EC action before crediting EU institutional signals — that asymmetry would systematically under-weight EP findings relative to EC findings.
4. **Conservative magnitude per post-apply discipline.** Slovakia received -5.5 on May 22. The EP vote occurred May 20 (2 days BEFORE that May 22 apply was filed in the canonical pipeline). Technically, the EP vote post-dates the May 22 evidence window only because the assessor cycle ran on May 22 evidence and missed the May 20 EP vote. The fairest reading: the May 22 -5.5 apply ENCODED the Liberties Dismantler classification but did NOT encode the EP vote. The EP vote is a NEW, independent institutional escalation.

**Magnitude selected: -2.0.** Calibration:
- Below the -5.5 first-baseline Dismantler apply already scored.
- Reflects "Tier-1.5" treatment — not yet a Commission formal investigation (which would justify a larger delta), but stronger than accumulator-only.
- Conservative per post-apply discipline: Slovakia just received -5.5 three days prior.
- Per second-event-at-floor convention precedent: when an entity has just been formally applied, distinct categorical second events warrant -2 to -3 range. EP conditionality urging is categorically distinct from Liberties Dismantler classification (one is Tier-1 academic/NGO, the other is Tier-1 institutional/governmental).
