## Context for this cycle

OCHA Situation Report (June 5/6, 2026): **936 fatalities and 2,903 injuries** since the October 10, 2025 ceasefire; daily meal provision fell from ~1.5M (mid-March) to ~678,000 (May 28) — roughly a 55% drop; Zikim crossing closed; UNRWA operations obstructed.

**Attribution note (critical):** These harms are caused by Israel's conduct (aid restrictions, crossing closures, military actions), not by the Palestinian Authority itself. Per the methodology, this evidence drives the **Israel** assessment, not Palestine's. For Palestine — a victim-population governing entity with severely constrained capacity under occupation/blockade — the new data reinforces, but does not lower, the existing score. The PA continues to operate diminished services under blockade.
