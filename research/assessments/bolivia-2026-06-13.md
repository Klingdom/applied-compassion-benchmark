## Context for this cycle

Fresh in-window evidence (Euronews, June 3, 2026; ACLED): the political/economic crisis is escalating — "about 100 road blockades... nearly double the number two weeks ago"; the defence and education ministers resigned on June 3; roadblocks have "triggered shortages of food, medicine and fuel" in La Paz, El Alto and other cities; the government "refused to rule out declaring a state of emergency and using the military." This represents a governance breakdown directly reducing access to essentials (notably medicine) for the population.
