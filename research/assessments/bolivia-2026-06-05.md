# Bolivia — Compassion Benchmark Reassessment (2026-06-05)

## Summary

Bolivia entered a 6th consecutive week of nationwide protests against the worst economic crisis in four decades, rooted in a land-mortgage law that triggered an indigenous/worker uprising. The June 2026 evidence is materially NEW state-attributable harm: 4 protesters killed, 127+ detained, and terrorism / narco-funding charges filed against COB labor-federation executive secretary Mario Argullo and 24 other union leaders. The Defense and Education Ministers resigned (June 2). This is not priced chronic fragility — it is a STATE-REPRESSION-IN-FLUX signal revealing un-priced ACC/BND/EQU/EMP/INT harm. Defensible downgrade -5.6 (28.4 -> 22.8), remaining in Developing.

## Methodology Ruling

STATE-REPRESSION-IN-FLUX (SCORABLE). Bolivia is a non-floor Developing entity; the directly-attested deaths and detentions (Tier 2-3) are scorable harm, distinct from the filed-but-unadjudicated corporate matters and the conflict-driven floors. The criminalization of labor leadership (AB1 deflection / AB4 weaponization) compounds ACC and INT. AWR/ACT/SYS held: ministerial resignations indicate a functioning-but-stressed apparatus, not collapse. No band crossing.
