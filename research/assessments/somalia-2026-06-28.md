# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Somalia

**Assessment date:** 2026-06-28
**Composite:** 4.7/100 (CONFIRM — threshold-check)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
The end-of-June date the IPC had flagged as a possible famine threshold for Buur Hakaba district has now passed. Buur Hakaba is at the worst malnutrition level the IPC measures (Phase 5, 37.1% acute malnutrition). But the scored trigger is a FORMAL IPC famine declaration — and as of June 28 no formal declaration has been issued. So this is a deepening of an already-counted crisis, not a new threshold crossing.

## §3e-bis Screening — threshold-check (decisive)
- **Baseline provenance:** Published 4.7 (AWR 1.5, EMP 1.5, ACT 1.5, EQU/BND/ACC/SYS/INT 1.0). Confirmed 4.7 yesterday.
- **Scored trigger NOT met:** Verified via IPC/FSNAU and FEWS NET — Buur Hakaba has exceeded ONE of three IPC famine thresholds (GAM 37.1%, AMN Phase 5) with "risk of Famine under a plausible worst-case scenario," but **no formal IPC famine declaration exists as of Jun 28.** The threshold date passing is not the declaration.
- **Double-count:** The deteriorating food-insecurity picture (≈6M / 1-in-3 at IPC Phase 3+) is a compounding shock on the already-priced near-floor crisis; re-scoring it now would double-count.
- **Attribution:** Drivers include drought, aid cuts, and conflict; the governing-actor own-conduct share (service collapse, most malnutrition facilities closed) reinforces but does not newly cross the near-floor.

## Within-window evidence
- **IPC/FSNAU + FEWS NET (Jun 2026, Tier 4-5):** Buur Hakaba AMN Phase 5, GAM 37.1%, one famine threshold exceeded; ~6M at Phase 3+; no formal declaration. https://www.ipcinfo.org/ipcinfo-website/where-what/eastern-africa/somalia/en/
- **Action Against Hunger / Crisis Group (Jun 2026, Tier 2-3):** famine risk emerging in Burhakaba amid aid cuts; WFP reaching ~1-in-10.

## Key Findings
- The famine threshold DATE passed, but the scored trigger (a formal IPC famine declaration) has NOT occurred — confirm, no move.
- Compounding shock on an already-priced near-floor crisis; double-count screened.
- Confirm 4.7, delta 0.0, no proposal.
- **Forward trigger:** a FORMAL IPC famine declaration for Buur Hakaba/Baidoa would warrant immediate re-assessment.
