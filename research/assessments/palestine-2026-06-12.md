## Context

Palestine (priority 65) governs amid catastrophic external constraints. Per OCHA/UNRWA: a fragile ceasefire (since Oct 10, 2025) with 700+/856 Palestinians killed since; 549 killed/4,066 injured accessing food (MoH); 43,000+ life-changing injuries; a 2026 Flash Appeal for $4B (3.6M people) only partially funded. Governance capacity is overwhelmed by occupation, blockade, and destruction. The profile confirms the low end of Developing, marginally below published 25.0.
