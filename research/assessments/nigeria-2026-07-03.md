# Assessment: Nigeria — Record 36.2M Food Insecure, Lean-Season Peak (Double-Count / Non-State Screen)

## Why it matters (plain language)
Nigeria's food crisis hit a record: 36.2 million people are food insecure, and 15,000 are at IPC Phase 5 "Catastrophe" in Borno State. WFP can reach only 740,000 of 6.2 million people in need in the northeast.
