## Why it matters (plain language)
Nigeria's hunger numbers hit their worst level in a decade this window. But the June crossing that drove them was already scored, and the new attacks fueling displacement come from armed insurgent groups, not the Nigerian state.

## Baseline provenance (§3e-bis(1))
