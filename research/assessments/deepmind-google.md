- **DeepMind positive record** — AlphaFold protein-folding release (open-science), AlphaMissense, medical AI work with research partners

- **ICO/Data protection challenges in EU** — ongoing

The 60-MP letter is a Tier-4 evidence signal: 60 members of a national legislature formally naming a specific commitment violation. SYS remains strong due to genuinely systemic AI safety research. **Composite 65.0 vs. published 81.4 = -16.4 delta; band shift: exemplary → established.**
