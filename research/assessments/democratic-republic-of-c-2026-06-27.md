# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Democratic Republic of Congo

**Assessment date:** 2026-06-27
**Composite score:** 2.3/100 (CONFIRM — near-floor reinforced)
**Band:** Critical

## Headline (plain language)

Why it matters: DRC scores 2.3 of 100. An Ebola outbreak now accelerating to 1,155 cases and 304 deaths — the fastest-growing in African history — reinforces a near-floor score that already reflects a state unable to protect its people.

## §3e-bis Screening + Attribution
- **Baseline provenance:** Published 2.3 (AWR 1.125, EMP 1.0, ACT 1.1, EQU 1.0, BND 1.1, ACC 1.1, SYS 1.1, INT 1.2). Reconstructs to 2.3.
- **Attribution:** M23 mass-killing harm (1,500+ civilians killed since December) is attributed to Rwanda and is scored against Rwanda, not DRC. The Ebola-response failure (45% contact tracing vs 90% needed) is DRC's own conduct and is scorable against DRC.
- **Directionality:** Within-window evidence is negative; the worsening outbreak reinforces the near-floor but is a continuation of an already-priced compounding crisis — no new threshold crossing. No positive countervailing own-conduct evidence.

## Within-window evidence
- **WHO / ECDC (Jun 25, 2026, Tier-5):** 1,155 confirmed cases / 304 deaths (up from 1,118/291 on Jun 24); first EU case (France, Jun 24); Germany imported case; Bundibugyo strain, no approved vaccine; PHEIC since May 16; contact tracing only 45%; Ituri 1,054 of 1,155 cases. https://www.who.int/emergencies/situations/ebola-outbreak---drc-2026
- **ECDC (Jun 2026, Tier-5):** Outbreak data and European case confirmation. https://www.ecdc.europa.eu/en/ebola-outbreak-democratic-republic-congo-and-uganda
- **Human Rights Watch (Jun 17, 2026, Tier-3):** Washington Accords unimplemented — Rwandan troops not withdrawn, FDLR not disbanded; 1,500+ civilians killed since December (M23/Rwanda conduct). https://www.hrw.org/news/2026/06/17/trump-declared-peace-in-congo-this-is-the-reality

## Key Findings
- The accelerating Ebola outbreak and the state's own response failure (low contact tracing) reinforce the near-floor across AWR, ACT, SYS and INT.
- M23 mass killings are attributed to Rwanda; they do not move DRC's score down further on their own.
- Two compounding catastrophic crises already priced; no new threshold crossing. Confirmed at 2.3. No change proposal.
