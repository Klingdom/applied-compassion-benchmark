## Methodology Note — Voluntary Buyout Weighting

Voluntary buyout programs with generous formulas (Rule of 70 = age + tenure ≥ 70 qualifies) and full benefits preservation are typically lighter-weight than mass forced layoffs. They represent:
- EMP-negative weight: workforce reduction during AI-driven headcount shift.
- EMP-positive offset: employee agency (opt-in), generous terms, no forced separation.

Net EMP weighting: marginal sub-threshold negative. The 20,000 combined Meta/Microsoft cut announcement is more material than the voluntary buyout itself, but framing as "AI labor crisis" rather than retaliatory-firing pattern keeps the magnitude below apply threshold.

The No Tech for Apartheid coalition signal is an external-accountability sub-threshold positive — workers organized at scale to demand corporate ethics on Pentagon AI contracts. Microsoft's prior responsiveness (Israel arc restructuring) suggests partial accountability uptake.

**Net direction:** sub-threshold accumulator remains -1.9. No apply this cycle.
