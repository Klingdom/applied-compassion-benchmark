# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Venezuela (Confirmation)

**Assessment date:** 2026-07-11 | **Published composite:** 18/100 (Critical) | **Recommendation:** confirm

## Within-window signal

Response mixed: interim government announced a $200M reconstruction fund and requested $4.85B from the IMF; the US committed $300M+ in aid. But open reporting shows authorities still absent in hard-hit areas (Catia la Mar), a widening health crisis in crowded shelters lacking clean water, and untreated injuries. (Al Jazeera Jul 7; EMS1 Jul; WMNF Jul 9.)
