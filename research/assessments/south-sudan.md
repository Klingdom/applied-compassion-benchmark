## Trigger for Reassessment

South Sudan is experiencing escalating violence at a scale not seen since the 2018 Revitalised Peace Agreement. OHCHR issued two emergency statements in April 2026: (1) April 2 — UN experts urge immediate protection of civilians amid escalating crisis (267,000+ displaced in Jonglei alone in 2026); (2) April 9 — escalating violence has created a child trafficking crisis. UN Security Council expected to vote on renewing the UNMISS mandate before April 30 expiry. Never assessed before.
