## Floor Protocol Notation

April 30 UNSC Resolution 2824 (13-0-2; China and Russia abstaining) extended UNMISS mandate through April 30 2027 BUT cut troop ceiling to 12,500. Pakistan publicly warned the reduction was unjustified given deteriorating security. The combination of reduced peacekeeping capacity and reignited civil war is a structural change in international protection framework — but cannot move composite below floor.

## New Conduct Categories Notated

1. **Civil war reignition** with 267,000+ displaced in Jonglei state alone in 2026.
2. **UNMISS troop ceiling reduction** to 12,500 concurrent with security deterioration.
3. **Year-long denial of food access** in Nasir County opposition-held areas.
4. **Drone strikes on civilian areas** as method of warfare.
5. **Humanitarian-access denial** as deliberate state strategy: NGOs directed to relocate to government-controlled territory.

## Cumulative Suffering Scale

- ~10 million people need life-saving humanitarian assistance.
- Government denying humanitarian access to opposition-held areas.
- Revitalized Peace Agreement implementation deadlocked.

## Floor-Exit Criteria (sustained)
