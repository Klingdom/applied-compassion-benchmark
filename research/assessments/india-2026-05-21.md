# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: India (2026-05-21)

**Trigger:** May 6-8 forced Rohingya-into-sea incident (40 refugees), UN special investigator appointed, IWT non-compliance carry-forward, NGO/free-speech crackdown. Multiple simultaneous events.

## Triggering Evidence Window

- 2026-05-06 to 2026-05-08: Indian authorities detained 40 Rohingya refugees in New Delhi, transported them via naval ship to Myanmar maritime border, crew members allegedly beat them, forced them into the sea with life jackets. Survivors (children, women, elderly) swam ashore to Myanmar's Tanintharyi coast. (SCMP, rtvonline.com, UN special investigator statement.)
- 2026-04-21: HRW "Pakistan: Surge in Forced Returns of Afghan Refugees" — broader regional context.
- 2026-05-20: India ABSTAINED on UNGA ICJ climate resolution (neutral signal among 28 abstaining countries).
- 2026-05: Foreign-funded NGOs barred from publishing news; Nagpur police arrest of 26-year-old for Instagram criticism of Operation Sindoor.
- 2026 (carry-forward): IWT (Indus Waters Treaty) deliberate non-compliance with binding international arbitration — methodology candidate active.
- HRW April 2026 Country Chapter documents Modi-era crackdown patterns, communal violence pattern, Muslim and Christian persecution.
