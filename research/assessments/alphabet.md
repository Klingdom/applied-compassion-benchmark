### Score Difference Analysis

The 9.4-point downgrade is driven by three concurrent developments: (1) courts found both search and ad-tech monopolies illegal, with DOJ and 38 states appealing for stronger remedies including Chrome/Android divestitures; (2) mass advertiser arbitration seeking potentially $218B in damages filed April 2026; (3) DEI targets eliminated and 58 DEI-related nonprofits defunded in February 2025 under political pressure. The published score likely predated the full antitrust remedy phase and DEI rollback. Current evidence supports a score at the lower end of the Functional band.

### Recommendation

Published score of 51.6 appears overstated. Current evidence supports approximately 42.2 (Functional band, but at the developing boundary). Recommend downgrade.
