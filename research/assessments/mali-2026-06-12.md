## Context

Mali (priority 80) is at the center of OCHA's June 2026 Sahel crisis: 24.3M people across the region need aid; Mali specifically shows a 64% increase in famine-level conditions in numerous areas and 1.5M severely food insecure. Central Sahel has ~12,900 schools closed. Junta governance, civilian abuses by both armed groups and state forces, and the conflict reaching the Atlantic coast confirm a deeply Critical profile, slightly below the published 12.5.
