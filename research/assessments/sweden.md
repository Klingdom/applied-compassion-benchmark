## Context

Rotation-backfill first assessment. Published composite 87.5. Key 2025-2026 evidence:
- Universal healthcare, education, social insurance
- Major migration policy tightening: asylum immigration at lowest since 1985; $34K repatriation grant introduced ([JPost](https://www.jpost.com/international/article-820110)); labour-immigration wage floor raised to 90% median
- EU Pact on Migration and Asylum implementation underway 2026
- Healthcare/schools/social services exempted from reporting obligation for children ([AIDA report](https://asylumineurope.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/AIDA-SE_2024Update.pdf))
- Continued NATO integration; pacified posture
- Refugee women/girls program cited as good practice by ECRE

**Drag on score:** Sharp migration policy tightening has raised concerns from ECRE/UNHCR about refugee welfare; asylum numbers dramatically reduced; repatriation incentives for beneficiaries of subsidiary protection. This represents modest EQU/EMP erosion without reaching structural failure.
