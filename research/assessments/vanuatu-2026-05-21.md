# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Vanuatu (2026-05-21)

**Trigger:** UNGA ICJ Climate Resolution ADOPTED 141-8-28 on 2026-05-20. Vanuatu was the lead sponsor and driving force of the resolution from 2019 ICJ AO conception through 2026 UNGA follow-up operationalization.

## Triggering Evidence Window

- 2026-05-20: A/80/L.65 adopted 141-8-28. Vanuatu lead sponsor. First operationalization of binding state climate obligations under the July 2025 ICJ Advisory Opinion. Foreign Minister Ralph Regenvanu: "victory for communities on the frontlines of the climate crisis."
- The resolution affirms states have binding legal duties to prevent and repair climate harm, just transition away from fossil fuels, and recognizes legal entitlements of sea-level-rise-affected states.
- Vanuatu invested 6+ years in coalition-building (PISFCC student-led campaign → ICJ AO → UNGA follow-up). Borne real diplomatic cost from fossil-fuel-exporting partners.
