## Assessment basis & attribution

Window event: Jun 20-21 Russian strikes on Poltava, Kharkiv (9 casualties), Kherson (25 civilians injured in 24h); 66 attacks on Ukrainian positions Jun 21. UN cumulative: 62,716 civilian casualties through May 2026 (16,126 killed, 46,590 injured); June 2026 the highest civilian-fatality month in 3 years.

**Attribution:** The civilian casualties are caused by **Russian strikes** — harm inflicted BY an external aggressor ON Ukraine and its population. Per the victim/perpetrator rule, these casualties are attributed to Russia, NOT scored against Ukraine. What is scorable is Ukraine's OWN conduct toward those it serves: civil-defense, evacuation, humanitarian coordination, treatment of vulnerable populations, and wartime governance integrity.

**Why confirmation.** The published 50 (Functional) already reflects a state maintaining substantial civilian-protection and governance functions under extreme duress, balanced against wartime constraints on equity (EQU 2.5 — occupied territories, displaced populations) and accountability. The window evidence is a continuation of the aggression against Ukraine, not new adverse Ukrainian conduct. Score confirmed at 50.
