## Key Findings

- **Codified impunity escalation (NEW, structural).** President Paz signed a law June 8, 2026 authorizing the military to use force against protesters with a "presumption of legality" — soldiers' actions deemed lawful unless proven otherwise. This is a durable state-conduct change, not merely incident-level harm, degrading accountability and integrity. (Al Jazeera; JURIST; the-messenger.com)
- **Escalated toll.** Independent ombudsman: 10 killed, 37 injured, 365 arrested (May 1–June 2); 7 deaths attributed by government to lack of medical attention amid blockades. Further arrests post-signing (28 El Alto, 23 Cochabamba). (Al Jazeera; ABC News)
- **Criminalization of dissent.** Terrorism charges against 25 union leaders incl. COB chief Mario Argullo. La Paz and El Alto near-fully blockaded; cities isolated. Worst economic crisis in four decades; Defense + Education ministers resigned June 2.
