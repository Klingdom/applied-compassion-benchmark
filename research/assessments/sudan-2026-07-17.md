Source: https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/07/1167871 ; https://www.ungeneva.org/en/news-media/news/2026/07/120309/sudans-people-subject-relentless-drone-strikes-el-obeid

- El Obeid holds more than half a million residents plus at least 100,000 internally displaced people; the city has been without electricity for nearly a month and food prices are up 300%. The UN High Commissioner has called for strong action at the highest level to prevent atrocity crimes. Source: https://www.ohchr.org/en/statements-and-speeches/2026/07/high-commissioner-turk-calls-strong-action-highest-level-prevent
