# Assessment: DR Congo — Ebola 4th Province (Near-Floor / Exogenous-Disease Screen)

**Composite:** 2.3/100 (CONFIRM)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
The Ebola outbreak (Bundibugyo strain, no approved vaccine or treatment) has reached a 4th province, Haut-Uele, as of June 29: 1,333 confirmed cases and 399 deaths, with an imported case detected in France and 20 cases in Uganda. The epidemic is a disease outbreak — exogenous — and DR Congo's weak response reinforces the near-floor 2.3 but compounds already-priced crises rather than crossing a new threshold. M23 conflict harm is attributed to Rwanda-backed forces (external/non-state).
