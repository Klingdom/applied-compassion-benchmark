# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Sudan (Floor Confirmation)

**Published composite:** 0.0/100 (Critical, harm-flag floor) — rank 190, countries index.
**Determination:** CONFIRM at 0.0. Floor sustained and substantively reinforced by a materially new international-legal finding.

## Within-Window Evidence — NEW: formal genocide-markers finding + El Obeid inquiry

The Human Rights Council-appointed **Independent International Fact-Finding Mission for Sudan** reported (Jul 8–9) that RSF mass killings, abductions and gang rape in **El Fasher** — where over 6,000 people were killed in three days as the city fell in October 2025 — bear "distinct markers of genocide," and warned the RSF is now deploying the same tactics around **El Obeid**: encircling the city, striking critical infrastructure, and restricting access to essential services. The Mission launched an **urgent inquiry** into the emerging El Obeid pattern ([OHCHR, 2026-07-08](https://www.ohchr.org/en/press-releases/2026/07/sudan-mass-killings-abductions-and-gang-rape-carried-out-el-fasher?sub-site=HRC); [Al Jazeera, 2026-07-09](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/7/9/un-probe-finds-mass-killings-gang-rapes-by-sudans-rsf-amount-to-genocide); [UN News](https://news.un.org/en/story/2026/07/1167897)).

This is a materially new, formally-dated escalation in the **international legal characterization** of the conflict, beyond the previously-confirmed El Obeid drone-strike toll and the European Parliament's RSF terrorist-designation call.

## Attribution Note

The RSF is a non-state paramilitary; the El Fasher/El Obeid atrocities are principally non-state harm and are the direct subject of the genocide-markers finding. Sudan's floor (0.0) rests on the Sudanese **state's / SAF's own-conduct** record — indiscriminate warfare, obstruction of humanitarian access, atrocity-scale civilian harm. The within-window evidence reinforces the atrocity environment and evidences no countervailing own-conduct improvement.

## Determination

Already at the harm-flag floor (0.0); floor arithmetic forecloses any downward move, and directionally negative evidence forecloses any upward move. **CONFIRM 0.0.** Floor sustained. The Fact-Finding Mission's genocide-markers finding is logged as a significant evidentiary milestone that strongly reinforces the floor even though no numeric move is possible.
