The assessed composite of 51.2 is 11.2 points below the published 62.5. No single dimension collapsed; every dimension simply landed between 2.8 and 3.4 raw where the placeholder had assigned a uniform 3.5. Systemic Thinking (assessed 3.4) and Action (assessed 3.4) come closest to the placeholder, supported by Fidelity's multi-year financial-mobility and K-12 financial education commitments. Empathy (assessed 2.8), Equity (assessed 2.8) and Integrity (assessed 2.8) fall furthest, driven by the return-to-office mandate and by an absence of published disaggregated equity outcome data rather than by any documented harm.

## Key Findings

- Fidelity handled its data breach reasonably well, which is why this is not a downgrade. It detected the intrusion itself, and the settlement offers up to $5,000 for documented losses, about $100 per person, and two years of identity protection with $1 million in fraud insurance.
- Fidelity invests upstream in the problem it profits from. Its financial-mobility and Fidelity Financial Forward programmes put financial education into under-resourced schools and communities, which lifts Systemic Thinking to the top dimension.
- The five-day office mandate is the main negative in the window. About 6,200 Boston staff and employees at three other hubs lose hybrid work from September 2026, and readers described it as "incredibly disruptive."
- The 11.2 point gap is a measurement artifact, not a decline. Fidelity's placeholder score gave it a flat 3.5 in all eight areas; a real 40-part review found normal variation between 2.8 and 3.4.
- Fidelity is private, so there is less to verify. No proxy statement, no workforce demographic disclosure, and no independent audit of its equity outcomes were available, which caps confidence across several dimensions.

## Strongest Dimensions

Systemic Thinking (raw 3.4) and Action (raw 3.4). Root Cause Orientation, Long-Term Impact and Coalitional Compassion all score 4 of 5: Fidelity has sustained a twenty-year partnership with Common Impact supporting underserved communities and funds financial education as an upstream intervention rather than only a marketing channel.

## Weakest Dimensions

Empathy (raw 2.8), Equity (raw 2.8) and Integrity (raw 2.8). Perspective-Taking, Internal Consistency and Boundaries Self-Sustainability all score 2 of 5, reflecting a major workplace policy change imposed on thousands of employees, including relocated staff and working parents, without evidence of structured consultation.

## Evidence Gaps

Fidelity is privately held, which is the dominant limitation here. There is no proxy statement, no SEC-mandated disclosure of workforce composition or executive-to-median pay, and no independent third-party audit of its diversity or customer-outcome data. Bias Awareness (EQ3) and Historical Harm Acknowledgment (EQ5) are scored 2 on absence of evidence, not on evidence of failure. Employee sentiment evidence is limited to press coverage of reader reactions rather than a systematic survey. Eleven of the forty subdimensions carry low confidence.

## Calibration Note

THIS IS A MEASUREMENT GAP, NOT A DECLINE. Fidelity Investments has never been individually assessed; its published 62.5 is a bulk-import placeholder that assigned a flat 3.5 to all eight dimensions. This first 40-subdimension review produces a dimension average of about 3.05, and essentially the entire -11.2 point gap comes from that arithmetic difference. The within-window evidence is mild: a $2.5 million settlement of a 2024 data breach affecting about 77,000 people, resolved with genuinely reasonable remedies, and a five-day return-to-office mandate. Neither is a compassion failure of the kind that would justify an 11-point drop. This is the documented scrutiny bias described in research/US_STATES_REASSESSMENT_RUNBOOK.md: adversarial review finds gaps that flat batch assignment never looked for. Recommendation is flag-for-review rather than downgrade, because the correct remedy is a cohort-wide calibration of never-assessed Fortune 500 placeholders, not a one-off cut to Fidelity while its actual conduct record for the period is unremarkable to good.

## Recommendation
