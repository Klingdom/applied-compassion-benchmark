## Why it matters (plain language)
Nigeria is in the worst hunger season it has ever recorded: more than 35 million people are at crisis-level hunger (IPC Phase 3+), and over 15,000 in Borno State are at the catastrophic Phase 5. This is real and severe — but it is the same crisis that already drove Nigeria's June 19 score cut to 18.0. Re-scoring the same evidence would double-count it. There is no materially new development for June 29. The score holds.
