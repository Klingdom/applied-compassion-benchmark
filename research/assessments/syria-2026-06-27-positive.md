# Special Upward Assessment: Syria

**Composite:** 0.0/100 (CONFIRM — floor holds; no upgrade warranted)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
Since Assad fell, more than half a million refugees have returned and a transitional-justice commission is meeting. Those are real and hopeful. But Syria's score of 0 is not a leftover from the Assad era. It was confirmed on June 5, 2026 — after the transition — because the new government's own forces and allied militias have carried out mass sectarian killings, and no one has been held to account.

## Symmetric-rigor screens
- **Stale-baseline screen (FIRES — §3e-bis(2)):** The deep-scan's upgrade case is "Syria at 0 by definition has room to move up… likely from the Assad period." That rationale is INVALID. The 0.0 floor was set/confirmed **2026-06-05**, post-Assad, on the *transitional government's own conduct*.
- **Ongoing own-conduct atrocity (DECISIVE):** UN experts found the July 2025 Sweida violence killed ~1,000, including at least 539 Druze civilians (39 women, 21 children), with at least 196 documented extrajudicial executions and 33+ villages burned, amid government deployment. The HRW "Are you Alawi?" report documents identity-based killings during the transition. The US State Department (early 2026) documents continuing sectarian assassinations, deaths under torture in state-run detention, and abductions of minority women. HRW (Jan 2026) found the transitional government failed to deliver justice for Sweida — a pattern of impunity.
- **Directionality screen (FIRES):** Surfaced on a positive signal (refugee returns) but the dominant within/near-window own-conduct evidence is continued mass harm and codified impunity. Positive process (a justice commission, EU funding) does not offset active atrocity at the floor.
- **Realization/attribution:** Refugee returns are realized but are not the government providing care; EU €175M is external (announced). Neither lifts a harm-threshold floor.

## Disposition
CONFIRM at 0.0. Delta 0.0. No change proposal. The floor is a harm-threshold designation grounded in the current government's own ongoing conduct; it cannot rise while mass sectarian killing and impunity persist. If a calibration question exists about floor entities with active transitions, that is a coordinator-level **flag-for-review**, not a one-off upgrade.
