## Attribution Analysis

The rising Ebola toll is disease-attributed (natural epidemic), not a discrete new own-conduct compassion failure by the DRC state. The launch of a clinical trial (2026-07-02) is a marginally positive response signal but far too small to move a near-floor score. No new discrete own-conduct atrocity or governance collapse beyond the already-priced baseline.
