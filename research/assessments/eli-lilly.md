- **ACT 68.8 (Action):** Manufacturing-scale therapeutic delivery (insulin volume; Mounjaro/Zepbound supply ramping; oncology pipeline; Connecting Hearts Abroad volunteer program; LLY Foundation). Established-floor on action delivery.

- **ACC 37.5 (Accountability):** Streck case is documented misreporting confirmed by jury and 7th Circuit; SCOTUS petition is affirmative legal action seeking to dismantle the federal mechanism that produced this accountability. Developing-floor on the accountability dimension itself.
- **SYS 68.8 (Systems):** Established-floor. Genuine therapeutic-access mission infrastructure (manufacturing scale, R&D pipeline, donations). Distinguishes Lilly from Danaher and BD on substantive systems.
- **INT 43.8 (Integrity):** Lowest dimension.
