# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Myanmar (Floor Confirmation)

**Assessment date:** 2026-07-11 | **Published composite:** 0.0/100 (Critical, floor) | **Recommendation:** confirm

## Within-window signal
Junta air and naval attacks on civilians continued through the window. On July 11, at least 22 displaced people were killed by a military airstrike on a monastery in Lin Ta Lu village, Sagaing Region, where 150+ people were sheltering. On July 8, four junta warships fired ~87 naval shells at Gwa Township, injuring six civilians including a five-year-old. On July 1, air force jets bombed a Muslim village (Kyetmauk Taung) in Buthidaung, Rakhine. (Mizzima Jul 10; Narinjara Jul; Fortify Rights.)

## Threshold determination
Ongoing own-conduct atrocity — deliberate aerial/naval bombardment of civilian sites including a monastery sheltering displaced people. Myanmar is already at the absolute floor (0.0); no lower move possible. Evidence reinforces the floor.

## Outcome
**Confirm at 0.0 (Critical, floor). Floor sustained/reinforced.**
