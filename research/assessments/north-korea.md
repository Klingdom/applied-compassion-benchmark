## Trigger for Reassessment

HRW March 19, 2026 report explicitly links North Korea's nuclear weapons program to human rights abuses — arbitrary detention, torture, forced labor, enforced disappearances — as integral mechanisms for funding and securing the weapons apparatus. UN Special Rapporteur Elizabeth Salmón told the HRC the situation "had showed no improvement and, in many instances, had degraded" over the past decade. South Korean government under President Lee Jae-myung weakened North Korea human rights policies in 2025.
