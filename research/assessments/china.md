## Dimension Details

### AWR 21.9
- A1: Large-scale poverty monitoring platforms (14-dept cross-check, early warning for rural medical-expense households). Substantive detection infrastructure for preferred populations.
- A2/A4: Detection systematically excludes Uyghur/Tibetan/Kazakh minority experiences; independent civil-society amplification channels suppressed.
- [China Daily poverty monitoring](https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202602/25/WS699e31c2a310d6866eb3a10f.html)

### EMP 18.8
- E1/E3: State capacity for affective resonance with preferred populations; systematic non-empathy toward minorities and dissidents (UN OHCHR Jan 2026).
- E5: Cultural empathy selectively applied; Tibetan and Uyghur cultural knowledge instrumentalized rather than centered.
- [OHCHR Jan 2026 press release](https://www.ohchr.org/en/press-releases/2026/01/un-experts-alarmed-reports-forced-labour-uyghur-tibetan-and-other-minorities)

### ACT 25.0
- AC4: Poverty elimination (lifted ~100M out of extreme poverty by 2020); 7M+ identified and supported in transition period. Measurable resource mobilization for rural Han populations.
- AC3: Efficacy within preferred domains is real; outside those domains (minorities, political dissidents) action is negative.

### EQU 12.5
- EQ1/EQ2: Two-tier system — universality for Han majority; systemic exclusion for Uyghur, Tibetan, Kazakh minorities and LGBTQ+ populations.
- EQ5: Historical harm acknowledgment floor — Cultural Revolution, Tiananmen, Xinjiang all officially denied.

### BND 18.8
- B5: Consent frameworks substantively absent — coercive labor-transfer programs, surveillance state compliance pressure.

### ACC 6.3
- AB1/AB2: Denial or minimization standard response to external documentation.
- AB3: Transparency limited to authorized data; independent journalism/NGO access restricted.
- AB5: No reparative action toward harmed populations; actively resists UN OHCHR engagement.

### SYS 25.0
- S1/S2: 5-year plans + 15th FYP (2026-30) routinized long-term planning. Poverty alleviation structural intervention.
- S4: Does not critique state structures producing minority harm.

### INT 9.4
