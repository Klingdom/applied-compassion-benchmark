# Special Upward Assessment: Colombia

## Why it matters (plain language)
One small armed faction in Colombia — the Comuneros del Sur, about 200-300 fighters — handed over weapons under the Total Peace policy. That is a real, if tiny, step. But analysts call it "symbolic more than substantive," the wider ELN peace talks have collapsed, and other armed groups are expanding. Colombia was confirmed at 35.9 just eleven days ago with no in-window escalation.
