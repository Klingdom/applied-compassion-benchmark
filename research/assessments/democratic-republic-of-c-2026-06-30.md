# Assessment: DR Congo — Ebola Spreads to 4th Province (Near-Floor Reinforcement)

**Composite:** 2.3/100 (CONFIRM — near-floor; escalation reinforces but does not clear a new threshold)
**Band:** Critical

## Why it matters (plain language)
Materially new June 30 data: the Bundibugyo-strain Ebola outbreak (DRC's 17th, started May) has spread to a **fourth province, Haut-Uele**, which borders South Sudan and CAR. Confirmed cases rose to **1,307 with 377 deaths** (from 1,155/304 on Jun 25); the DRC's entire northeast (~15M people) is now affected; Uganda has 19 cases. There is no vaccine or specific treatment for this strain. This is described as the fastest-growing Ebola outbreak ever.

## Baseline provenance (§3e-bis(1))
- Live baseline **2.3**, rank **181**, Critical. The near-floor 2.3 already prices DRC's compounding crises: the Ebola response weakness (mistrust, unsafe burials, low contact tracing), conflict-driven displacement, and severe state-capacity deficits across all dimensions (~1.0–1.2).

## The scorable question: DRC's own conduct
- The **disease spread itself** is largely epidemiological dynamics (funeral transmission, community mistrust), not deliberate government harm. The scorable element is the **response capacity** — already priced at near-floor.
- **M23 mass-killing harm** in the east is attributed to **Rwanda** (attribution), not DRC.

## §3e-bis screening
- **(3) Attribution:** M23 harm → Rwanda; epidemic dynamics are exogenous to deliberate own-conduct.
- **Near-floor limitation:** at 2.3, any downgrade toward 0.0 is bounded below 5 points; the escalation reinforces the near-floor rather than crossing a new threshold. A floor crossing would require documented deliberate government obstruction of the Ebola response (e.g., blocking WHO corridors).
- **(4) Double-count — NO move:** worsening of an already-priced crisis.

## Disposition
**CONFIRM at 2.3. Delta 0.0. No change proposal.** Confidence: high.
- **Watch — ACT/AWR:** cross-border spread (South Sudan/CAR/Uganda), contact-tracing collapse, M23-corridor obstruction.
- **Forward trigger (floor crossing):** documented deliberate government obstruction of the outbreak response.
