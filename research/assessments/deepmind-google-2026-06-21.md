## Assessment basis & attribution

Window event (Tier 3-4): On Jun 18, Google DeepMind published an "AI Control Roadmap" adapting MITRE ATT&CK to treat AI agents as potential insider threats, with a TRAIT&R taxonomy, detection tiers (D1-D4) and response tiers (R1-R3), and 15 system-level defenses. It analyzed ~1M coding-agent tasks (finding most issues stem from "overzealous agents, not malicious intent") and deployed a live monitor for the Gemini Spark agent. It signed a CAISI pre-deployment evaluation agreement; G7 Jun 17 coalition advocacy with Anthropic.

**Attribution:** This is DeepMind's OWN conduct — proactive safety/anticipatory-awareness work published voluntarily. Mildly positive.
