## Within-window signal
Bolivia is NEW to the priority list this cycle. Since May 2026, nationwide road blockades and protests against President Rodrigo Paz's austerity measures have produced escalating unrest. Reported blockade/protest-related deaths have risen (the scan cites 24; open sources document "at least 16" attributable to the blockades) and nearly $3B in economic losses. On/around July 7–9, police arrested Vicente Salazar, top leader of the La Paz "Túpac Katari" farmers' federation, in El Alto; prosecutors are seeking pretrial detention, alleging he organized the blockades. The federation declared an internal state of emergency and called protests over the arrest. (Wikipedia 2026 Bolivian protests; UPI Jul 7.)

Determination: **confirmation, not a scoreable ≥5pt move and not a band crossing.**
