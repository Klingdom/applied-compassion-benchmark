# Compassion Benchmark Assessment: Somalia

**Assessment date:** 2026-06-26
**Composite score:** 4.7/100 (CONFIRM — near-floor reinforced)
**Band:** Critical

## Headline (plain language)

Why it matters: Somalia already scores 4.7 out of 100, near the bottom. A district called Buur Hakaba is on the brink of famine, but as of today no formal famine has been declared. The crisis reinforces the existing near-bottom score.

## §3e-bis Screening + Attribution
- **Baseline provenance:** Published 4.7 (AWR 1.5, EMP 1.5, ACT 1.5, EQU 1.0, BND 1.0, ACC 1.0, SYS 1.0, INT 1.0). Reconstructs to 4.7.
- **Threshold check:** Verified with IPC/FSNAU — Buur Hakaba has surpassed the malnutrition famine threshold (GAM 37.1%, IPC Phase 5 for acute malnutrition) but there is NO formal IPC famine declaration. The IPC frames it as "risk of Famine," with a second failed crop season as the trigger. This is a compounding shock on an already-priced crisis, not a new threshold-crossing severity event.
- **Directionality:** No positive countervailing own-conduct evidence; near-floor headroom is small.

## Within-window evidence (near-floor reinforcement)
- **IPC / FSNAU (April-June 2026 update, Tier-5):** ~6M people (31% of analysed population) in IPC Phase 3+, up ~509,000 from prior projection; "risk of Famine in one location." Buur Hakaba GAM 37.1% has surpassed the famine malnutrition threshold; a second consecutive failed season would likely lead to famine. https://www.ipcinfo.org/ipcinfo-website/where-what/eastern-africa/somalia/en/
- **Action Against Hunger (Jun 2026, Tier-3):** Most malnutrition health facilities closed; food assistance in steep decline; famine risk emerging in Burhakaba. https://www.actionagainsthunger.org/press-releases/somalia-faces-deepening-hunger-crisis-as-risk-of-famine-emerges-in-burhakaba/
- **International Crisis Group (Jun 2026, Tier-3):** Somalia barrels toward possible famine amid aid cuts; WFP reaching only 1-in-10 in need; 1.9M children malnourished. https://www.crisisgroup.org/anb/africa/somalia/somalia-barrels-toward-possible-famine-amid-aid-cuts

## Key Findings
- Buur Hakaba is at famine RISK (no formal IPC declaration as of the assessment date); this reinforces but does not cross a new threshold beyond the already-priced near-floor.
- Aid-delivery collapse (WFP reaching 1-in-10) and facility closures reinforce ACT and AC4 weakness.
- Confirmed at 4.7. No change proposal (compounding shock on priced crisis; no formal famine declaration; verify FSNAU for any future declaration).
