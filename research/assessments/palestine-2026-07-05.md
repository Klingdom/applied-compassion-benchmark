# Assessment: Palestine — Ceasefire Violations + Aid Blockage (Attribution Screen)

**Composite:** 25.0/100 (CONFIRM — no scorable Palestinian-state own-conduct event this window)
**Band:** Developing

## Why it matters (plain language)
The suffering in this window is real and severe. But the benchmark scores an entity's OWN conduct toward the people it serves. Nearly all the documented harm this window was inflicted by Israel, not by Palestinian institutions — so it is scored against Israel, not Palestine.

## Baseline provenance (§3e-bis(1))
- Live baseline **25.0**, rank **123**, Developing. This already prices the collapse of Palestinian governance capacity, fragmented authority (PA/Hamas), and minimal ability to protect or provide for civilians under occupation and blockade.

## The scorable question: Palestine's own institutional conduct
- Israel killed 3 Palestinians in Gaza on Jul 1 despite the ceasefire; Al Jazeera documents 3,465 Israeli ceasefire violations and 1,053 Palestinians killed since the Oct 10 2025 truce; only 36% of allocated aid trucks (55,539 of 156,000) entered Gaza; Israel controls ~64% of Gaza. **All of this is Israel's conduct** — killings, aid obstruction, territorial control — and attributes to Israel (published 0.0), NOT to Palestine.
- No new within-window evidence of Palestinian-state own-conduct harm clearing a threshold, and no positive countervailing own-conduct (e.g., a documented equitable relief scale-up under Palestinian control) that would support an upgrade.

## §3e-bis screening
- **(3) Attribution:** the killings, the 3,465 violations, and the aid blockage are another state's (Israel's) conduct — cannot yield a downgrade of Palestine.
- **(3) Directionality:** entity surfaced on negative evidence attributable to another actor; absence of new own-harm is not positive evidence, so no upgrade either.
- **(4) Double-count:** governance collapse under occupation/blockade is already priced at 25.0.

## Disposition
**CONFIRM at 25.0. Delta 0.0. No change proposal.** Confidence: medium.
- **Forward trigger (downgrade):** independently documented Palestinian-authority deliberate targeting of, or systematic aid diversion from, its own civilians.
