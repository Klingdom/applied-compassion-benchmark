## Calibration Note — Mali/Burkina Faso Cross-Peer Flag (DO NOT UNILATERALLY RESOLVE)

The open cross-peer calibration flag (Mali 12.5 vs Burkina Faso 6.3) is sharpened this cycle: Burkina Faso had its UN human-rights office forcibly and permanently closed (Jul 2), while Mali retains OHCHR presence. This accountability-infrastructure asymmetry *supports* Burkina Faso sitting below Mali. Separately, Mali's uniform-1.5 baseline has never been re-derived against specific atrocity findings the way Burkina Faso's was — raising the question of whether Mali is under-assessed relative to its own conduct. This is a coordinator/meta-level calibration question. **Recommend flag-for-review; do not encode as a one-off Mali reset.**
