## Key Findings

- Why it matters: Federal records reported July 19–22, 2026 show Northwestern accepted funding from Chinese entities on US government watchlists — including ~$3M+ (2017–2021) from the military-linked, Treasury-listed Aero Engine Corporation of China.
- Northwestern did not respond to press requests for comment, and it continues to operate under a $75 million settlement with the Trump administration — both point to transparency and governance strain rather than direct harm to students or community.
- On the compassion framework, this is a governance/accountability signal, not a care-delivery failure; a full 40-subdimension review lands essentially unchanged from the published Functional score.
