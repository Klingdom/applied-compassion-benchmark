## Context: In-Window Event

On **13 June 2026**, Anthropic disabled all access to its Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models — for all foreign nationals, including non-citizen employees — to comply with a U.S. Commerce Department national-security export-control directive after officials cited a technique to bypass Fable 5's safeguards relating to Mythos's cybersecurity abilities. Anthropic published a notice apologizing to customers, stating it believed the action was a "misunderstanding," and publicly disagreeing that "the finding of a narrow potential jailbreak should be cause for recalling a commercial model deployed to hundreds of millions of people." The conduct shows transparency (prompt public notice) and compliance with government direction, alongside customer disruption. Net effect is consistent with the published Functional score.
