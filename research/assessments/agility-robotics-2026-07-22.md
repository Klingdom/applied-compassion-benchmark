## Key Findings

- Why it matters: Agility Robotics builds the Digit humanoid for warehouse work. On a suffering-reduction lens its distinguishing conduct is designing robots to be safe working directly alongside people, not fenced off from them.
- Digit v5 is being built to sense humans and work fence-free alongside warehouse staff - a genuine worker-safety positive (TechCrunch, July 17, 2026).
- Digit is in real revenue-generating deployment at Amazon, GXO, Schaeffler and Toyota, having moved 100,000 totes at one GXO site (TechCrunch, July 2026).
- The May proposal's ~17-point partnership-based drop is not substantiated; the rigorous review reproduces the established-band baseline.

## Strongest Dimensions

## Weakest Dimensions
