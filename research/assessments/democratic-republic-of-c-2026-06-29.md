**Assessment date:** 2026-06-29

## Why it matters (plain language)
The DRC is fighting its third-largest Ebola outbreak ever — 1,155 confirmed cases and 304 deaths as of June 25, from a strain with no vaccine or treatment, growing faster than any prior outbreak on record. Meanwhile M23 fighters, backed by Rwanda, continue mass killings and sexual violence, with 5.8 million people displaced. The M23 atrocities are Rwanda-attributed; the DRC's own weak outbreak response reinforces its near-floor 2.3. These are compounding, already-priced crises. The score holds.
