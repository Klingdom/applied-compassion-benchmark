## Context for this cycle

Two fresh, in-window signals:
1. **OBBBA Medicaid work-reporting interim final rule** — HHS released the interim final rule implementing "community engagement" (80 hrs/month) requirements; effective late-2026/Jan-1-2027; reporting widely cites CBO projection of ~7.8M losing Medicaid coverage over the budget window. (Center for American Progress timeline; rule released ~June 1-3, 2026.)
2. **ICE detention deaths and expansion** — ACLU (Apr 28, 2026) documents the 17th 2026 death (deaths roughly one every six days; 40+ since the campaign began) and a plan to convert 20+ warehouses, raising capacity to 96,600. KFF documents 46 deaths since Jan 2025 as of Mar 18, 2026. The scanner also cited an alleged June 4 memo halting reporting of deaths of released detainees (not independently confirmed in fetched sources — treated as unverified).

These deepen suffering for low-power populations (low-income Medicaid enrollees; detained migrants). They support a conservative downward nudge but the US is already deep in the Critical band, limiting composite movement.
