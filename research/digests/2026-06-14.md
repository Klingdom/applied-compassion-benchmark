The US sub-threshold deterioration is the digest's quiet signal. But the mechanism's limitation is the same as with Bolivia in June 12-13: sub-threshold accumulation does not generate proposals, which means the published score can age below its calibrated level for multiple cycles before a single qualifying event triggers the next formal apply.
