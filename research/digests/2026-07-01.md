## Methodology Notes

### Codified-Policy Attribution Rule (Applied: China Ethnic Unity Law)

The July 1 cycle applies the codified-policy attribution rule for the first time in the June/July series:
1. **In-force vs. pre-adjudication:** The Ethnic Unity and Progress Law is in force on July 1 — not pre-adjudication. The §3e-bis(1) pre-adjudication screen does not apply.
2. **Codification vs. new conduct:** Both Amnesty International and HRW characterize the law as codifying existing documented practices, not creating new ones. This triggers the "already-priced" test: if the harm the law authorizes was already scored in prior cycles, the in-force date does not independently generate a new dock.
3. **Marginal delta calculation:** The law's marginal new harm = the incremental codification effect beyond existing practice. That is approximately two quarter-step docks (EQU + EMP), yielding -1.5 composite — below the 5-point threshold.
4. **Forward trigger formalization:** Article 63 extraterritorial enforcement actions (prosecutions, renditions, family detentions of diaspora) are NOT codification of existing practice — they are genuinely new realized harm. These constitute the scored threshold event.
5. **Precedent:** This ruling establishes the "codification ≠ new threshold event" principle for cases where a law embeds already-documented conduct. It is distinct from a NEW law authorizing conduct not previously practiced — which would be scored immediately on first enforcement.

### First-Assessment Baseline (Applied: California)

California's first formal assessment follows the baseline-establishment procedure:
1. The canonical composite formula is applied to within-window evidence: base score from dimensional raw scores + integration premium (up to 10 points for consistent performance).
2. California's base 79.7 + premium 8.0 = 87.7. The premium reflects near-zero inter-dimension variance (all dimensions at or above 4.0/5.0).
3. Within-window positives and negatives are applied at the marginal level — neither the Housing Agency launch nor the homelessness data alone clears the 5-point threshold from 87.7.
4. Watch flags are opened for dimensions where medium-term pressure exists (ACC/EQU/ACT) without forcing an immediate dock.
5. This baseline is the authoritative starting point for all future California assessments.

### Exogenous-Event Attribution (Applied: Anthropic Model Ban Arc)

The Anthropic model ban/clearance/lift arc (June 12–30) is fully resolved as net-neutral under the exogenous-event attribution rule:
1. **Ban (June 12):** US government action (Commerce Department). Anthropic did not harm anyone; the government acted on cyber-capability concerns. Not scorable against Anthropic.
