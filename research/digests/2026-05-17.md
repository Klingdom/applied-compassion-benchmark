## Impacts and Insights

**Sub-threshold documentation as a methodological innovation.** The introduction of formal sub-threshold movement records (India –1.5, Pakistan +0.4) addresses a gap that emerged as the evidence base deepened: real events with clear directional implications that fall below the application threshold were previously lost between cycles. The sub-threshold record preserves trajectory signals for multi-cycle analysis without inflating the score-change count. This is particularly important for entities near boundaries (Pakistan at 17.6, 2.4pt below 20/21) where cumulative sub-threshold signals may eventually compound into a threshold-crossing event.

**Legal-defiance arc as a structural pattern.** The India-Pakistan IWT bifurcation suggests that international treaty disputes — where one state's conduct is the direct cause of another state's legal engagement — will increasingly generate cross-entity coupled assessments. This is a different assessment challenge than single-entity events: the causal chain connects the two scores, and the structural outcome (water insecurity for 240M people) is independent of which entity wins the legal exchange. The methodology now has a named category for this pattern.

**Floor-state differentiation without numerical movement.** Five floor confirmations in a single cycle, each with distinct methodology sub-anchors, demonstrate that the floor band is not a methodology dead-end. The category accumulation at Israel, Sudan, Myanmar, and Russia documents structurally different kinds of floor-state conduct: systematic IHL-protected-site targeting (Israel), starvation-as-warfare genocide (Sudan), educational institution targeting (Myanmar), and mass-drone residential attacks concurrent with IHL-compliant gestures (Russia). These distinctions will matter for any future methodology expansion that introduces sub-floor tiers.

**Published-score drift accumulating across multiple entities.** Three math-hygiene drift flags flagged this cycle: Meta Platforms (0.9pt), Oracle (0.3pt), United States (0.3pt). The Meta Platforms drift (0.9pt) is the largest active discrepancy in the Fortune 500 cluster outside the Open Bionics formula-audit CRITICAL BLOCKING.
