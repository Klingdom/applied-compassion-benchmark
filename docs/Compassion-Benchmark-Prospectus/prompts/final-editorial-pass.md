# Final Editorial Pass Prompt

Perform the final editorial pass on the manuscript.

Check:

- consistent title hierarchy
- no em dashes
- no public-facing ACB
- no public-facing Applied Compassion Benchmark
- no placeholder text
- no TODOs
- no unsupported claims left unflagged
- no generic chapter endings
- no repeated banned phrases
- no formatting inconsistencies

Produce:

- final cleaned manuscript
- final claims-to-verify list
- final completion summary
