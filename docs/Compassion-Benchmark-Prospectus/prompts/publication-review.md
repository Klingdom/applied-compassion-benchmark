# Publication Review Prompt

Review the manuscript as a professional editor preparing it for publication.

Assess:

- structure
- argument
- voice
- originality
- credibility
- funder readiness
- design readiness
- claim discipline
- internal consistency
- brand consistency

Create:

1. Summary judgment
2. Top 10 required revisions
3. Chapter-by-chapter issues
4. Claims to verify
5. Design/layout issues
6. Final readiness rating

Do not soften critique.
