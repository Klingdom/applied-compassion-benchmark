# Eliminate AI Slop Prompt

Your job is to aggressively remove AI-sounding writing.

Search for:

- generic insight
- recycled phrasing
- overly symmetrical paragraph structure
- unnecessary setup
- fake depth
- paragraph endings that sound like slogans
- inflated words
- empty claims
- "not X but Y" overuse
- repetitive rhetorical questions

Rewrite the passage with more specificity, stronger pacing, and fewer words.

If a sentence could appear in a generic nonprofit report, either make it specific or delete it.
