# Critique Chapter Prompt

Provide a direct editorial critique of the chapter.

Do not praise unless useful.

Identify:

- weak opening
- generic prose
- unsupported claims
- over-explaining
- missing concrete examples
- repetitive cadence
- vague public benefit
- unclear connection to Compassion Benchmark
- places that sound like AI
- sections that should be cut

Then provide a prioritized rewrite plan.

Do not rewrite unless asked.
