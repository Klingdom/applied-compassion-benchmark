# Compassion Benchmark

## Building the Global Standard for Measuring Institutional Compassion

### A Strategic Vision, Research Prospectus, and Funding Opportunity

---

**Cover tagline**

We measure institutions on almost everything except whether they reduce suffering.

[GRAPHIC: Full-bleed cover. The eight-dimension color ring as the hero mark over a dark field, with the five-band hairline beneath it. The calibrated arc states the whole 0 to 100 scale before a word is read.]

---

**Back cover**

Every domain of institutional performance now has an external standard: audits for the books, recalls for the products, benchmarks for the models. One question still has no comparable public measure. When an institution's decisions reach a person who is suffering, does it recognize that, respond proportionally, and reduce the harm, or not.

Compassion Benchmark is an independent benchmark institution built to answer that question in public, score by score, and to make those scores impossible to buy.
