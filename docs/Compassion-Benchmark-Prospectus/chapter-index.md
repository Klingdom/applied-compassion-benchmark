# Chapter Index: Compassion Benchmark Funder Prospectus

**Publication:** Flagship funder prospectus, Compassion Benchmark.
**Total target length:** 25,000–35,000 words (this plan budgets ~33,830 words across front matter, 30 chapters, and 6 appendices, inside the band, with the per-section figures set as **upper targets**; trim toward the lower end where the argument allows).
**Authoring rule:** Every chapter draws from `source_notes.md` (the single source of truth) and follows `style-lock.md`. Chapters run 800–1,500 words unless the purpose requires otherwise. Use **1,256** as the scored-entity count and **v1.2** scoring throughout (see source_notes §0). No em dashes; public brand is "Compassion Benchmark" only.
**Visual system:** one recommended visual per section; brand/accent is blue `#3b82f6`, cyan `#7dd3fc` reserved for the Exemplary band only. Final art governed by Appendix F and `docs/BRAND_VISUAL_IDENTITY.md` (dimension colors from `dimensions.ts`).
**Locked references:** Appendices A, B, C in `chapters/` are locked first and cited by the body (dimension codes, evidence tiers, lifecycle steps).
**Column key:** each row carries number, title, purpose, core reader takeaway, target words, key source material, recommended visual, engagement point (one device per chapter), and claims to verify (anticipated flags for the claims pass).
